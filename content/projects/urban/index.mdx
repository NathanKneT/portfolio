Urban refers to the characteristics of city life, where buildings, streets, and people merge into a dynamic landscape. It is a space of constant movement, energy, and diversity, where the rhythm of daily life unfolds in every corner. Urban environments capture the essence of human interaction, blending architecture and culture into a vivid, ever-changing scene.
